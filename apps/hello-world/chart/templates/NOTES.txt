1. Get the ALB DNS name for your application:
   kubectl get ingress {{ include "hello-world.fullname" . }} \
     --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} \
     -o jsonpath='{.status.loadBalancer.ingress[0].hostname}'

2. It may take 2-3 minutes for the ALB to provision and become healthy.

3. Test the application:
   curl http://<ALB_DNS>/
   curl http://<ALB_DNS>/health
